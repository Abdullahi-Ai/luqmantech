ABOUT LUQMAN CYBER

More Than Just a
Cyber Cafe

Luqman Cyber was founded with a simple mission: to bridge the digital divide in Namanga. In an era where government services, education, and business have moved online, many in our community were left behind due to a lack of access or technical know-how.

We stepped in to fill that gap. Today, Luqman Cyber is the trusted digital hub for hundreds of residents. We don't just "do typewriting"; we solve problems.

From helping a grandmother file her NIL Returns to ensure she keeps her pension, to assisting a high school graduate apply for HELB & University placement, we are with you at every important milestone.

Student Success

We have helped over 200+ students successfully apply for KUCCPS university placement and HELB loans.

Business Growth

Assisted 50+ local businesses in registering on eCitizen and becoming eTIMS compliant.

Saving Time

Residents no longer need to travel to Kajiado or Nairobi for passport or certificate applications.
