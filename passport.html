Our Services

Kenyan Passports

We assist with online application filling, invoice generation, and appointment booking for:

  • New Passports & Renewals
  • Lost/Mutilated Passport Replacement
  • Diplomatic Passport Assistance

Interstate Pass (Temporary Permit)

Traveling to Tanzania, Uganda, or Rwanda? Get your Temporary Permit processed instantly.

  • Fast & Easy Processing
  • Valid for East Africa Travel (1 Year)
  • Photo & ID Copy services available

Vehicle & Border Support

We help you navigate online portals for essential vehicle documents:

  • Temporary Vehicle Permit (C32): KRA Portal assistance.
  • COMESA Insurance: Online application guidance (Yellow Card).
  • Road Tolls: Online payment support.

Visa Applications

Accuracy is key for Visas. We help you fill and submit forms for:

  • USA (DS-160) & Canada Visitor Visas
  • UK & Schengen Visa Applications
  • East Africa Tourist Visa
  • eTA Kenya for incoming visitors

Immigration Permits

Assistance with eFNS portal applications for foreigners in Kenya:

  • Class G & D Work Permits
  • Dependent & Student Passes
  • Special Passes & Alien Cards

Professional Assistance
