eCitizen & eTIMS Services

Accessing government services has gone digital, but navigating the portals can be tricky. We provide seamless assistance for all eCitizen and eTIMS applications using our fast and secure network.

Key Services

  • eTIMS Compliance: We help businesses onboard to the new electronic tax invoice system, ensuring you can trade without disruption.
  • Good Conduct: Application for Police Clearance Certificates (Good Conduct) for job seekers.
  • Business Registration: Name search and registration of Business Names and Companies via eCitizen.
  • HELB Loans: First-time applications and subsequent loan processing for university students.
  • NTSA: Driving license renewals and vehicle transfer services.

Why Use Our Cyber?

We have high-speed scanners for document capitalization and extensive experience with government portals, creating fewer rejections and delays for your applications.
